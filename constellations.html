CONSTELLATIONS
3D SEQUENCE
For the first semester at ECAL we had to reimagine a painting/artwork from the 20th century as 3D artwork. The result were three sequences that capture different angles. My choice fell on “Ciphers and Constellations” by Joan Miró. I designed and animated the three sequences with Cinema4D. Later my project was repurposed to advertise for the workshop week at our school. The posters (printed and animated) were designed by Inés Barrionuevo.
Project led by Benjamin Muzzin
Programs used
