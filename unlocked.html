UNLOCKED
UNREAL ENGINE GAME
'Unlocked' is a video game that criticises societal norms in education. The main character is a girl who has yet to find her place in the world. She has pink skin and horns that represent her alienation from her environment, and always wears a melancholy expression. As players explore the game, they discover the gendered characteristics of schools, symbolised by colour-coded lockers and a scene in the classroom that represents public scrutiny. The library reveals the pervasiveness of the bullying culture, forcing the players to confront the pressures of society. The story deepens in a garden featuring caricatural sculptures embodying ideals of beauty. Players must destroy these symbols, revealing the school's attempts to conceal the influence they exert on pupils. This game is a commentary on societal expectations, gender norms and subtle coercion within educational institutions."
Project led by Valerio Meschi
