DIGITAL CLEAN UP DAY
3D VIDEO SEQUENCE
This project was created in collaboration with Livia Schmid during a bloc week at ECAL. The goal was to create a 3D video sequence that would be used to promote the Digital Clean Up Day. The video was created with Blender. We were heavily inspired by clay textures and the idea of representing our behavior towards digital pollution in a playful way.
Project lead by Angelo Benedetto and Vincent Jacquier
Programs used
Cinema 4D
Blender
After Effects
