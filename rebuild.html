DYNAMIC IDENTITY CONCEPT
In the second semester at ECAL, we had to conceptualise and design an identity for a fictitious festival. I designed an identity for an upcycling festival that promotes sustainability and the democratisation of design and crafts. The posters can be generated by a Javascript program that I coded. You can change the composition, background and sizes of the furniture to create different posters.
Project led by Angelo Benedetto
