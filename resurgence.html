RESURGENCE
3D SEQUENCE
For the first semester at ECAL we had to create a 3D sequence based on the topic PAST/FUTURE, a direct confrontation of two spaces in time in terms of their temporalities and dimensions. I chose to create a narrative of a god-like figure that is resurging from the past in the form of nature. The main goal for me was to achieve the most realistic look possible and work on textures and lighting.
